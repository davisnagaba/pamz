About Rukundo Pamella

Rukundo Pamella is a talented gospel artist and songwriter dedicated to spreading the message of God's love through music. Born and raised in Uganda, her musical journey began in the church choir at a young age.

With a passion for worship and a gift for songwriting, Rukundo has composed numerous inspirational songs that have touched lives across the nation. Her music blends contemporary gospel with traditional African rhythms, creating a unique sound that resonates with audiences of all ages.

Rukundo's ministry focuses on healing, hope, and the transformative power of faith. Through her music, she aims to bring people closer to God and inspire positive change in communities.
